It's the stuff of legend and myth. It's dark and mysterious. It's coffee.

People drink a lot of coffee. It's one of the most popular beverages in the world, it's the second most traded commodity in the world (oil is first)*, there are whole industries built around the stuff, and you probably don't understand it at all. Don't feel bad--most people don't. From the myths of Kaldi and his dancing goats to patently absurd claims about acid, coffee is steeped in misinformation, and one of the things I hope to teach here is that steeping coffee in anything is a terrible idea.

As an experienced barista and coffee roaster, I naturally have a lot to say about coffee, and a passion for debunking myths and rumors about my beverage of choice. Read on to learn all you ever wanted to know about coffee...and a bunch of stuff you thought you knew but were, in fact, totally wrong about.
