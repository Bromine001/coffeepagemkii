French Press Coffee

Equipment you will need for your coffee:

A French press, a cup or two, coffee, a kettle of boiling water. You will also need a plastic spoon or chopstick and a timer (not pictured).

I always reccomend weighing out your coffee whenever possible. This will lead to the most consistent results.

French press carafes will hold 32 ounces total. After the grounds are in, that leaves room for about 30 ounces water. I weigh out 1.8 ounces beans for a standard size French press.

Start by grinding fresh coffee coarsely.

Pour the grounds into your press.

Boil some water in your kettle, then give it a minute to cool from 212 to about 205. Then fill your press with hot water.

Give it a quick stir with a plastic or wooden utensil -- don't use a metal knife or spoon. Set a timer for 3 minutes.

Put the lid on, and adjust the plunger so that the grounds are held under the surface of the water.

When your time is up, gently but firmly push the plunger down, and pour.

Coffee!
